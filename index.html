My first contact with Machine Learning was during my Master Thesis work. It consisted in a modification of the K-means unsupervised clustering algorithm, that we modified updating the cluster centroids "on the fly" (single-pass k-means, Ordovás-Pascual & Sánchez Almeida 2014). Tested in a subsample of 20000 galaxy spectra, we reached a decrease of the computation time (between 20-40%) with respect to the K-means traditional approach, obtaining equivalent classifications.

Predicting the numeric rating of Amazon books review based exclusively on the text of the reviews

Given a text review, can we guess (from 1 to 5 stars) which is the rating of the review? We certainly can estimate it roughly. I downloaded from the Amazon Review Data (2018) the small book subset to perform this exercise. The objective is converting each review to TF-IDF vectors and to use a Machine Learning algorithm using these features to predict the numeric rating of the review.
